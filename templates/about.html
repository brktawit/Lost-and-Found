About Us

Who We Are

The Lost Item Reporting System is a digital tool designed for police stations, schools, companies, and other organizations to manage lost and found items efficiently. Our platform ensures that reported found items can be returned to their rightful owners through a structured and secure process.

Why We Built This Platform

Many institutions struggle to manage lost items, leading to confusion and unclaimed property. Our system provides a simple, organized, and transparent way for users to report found items while administrators oversee their safe return.

How It Works

  • Users: Report items they found, manage their reports (edit/delete), and specify item details.
  • Admins: Manage reported items, view all submissions, and facilitate item returns.
  • Security: Only registered users can report items, and only admins can verify and return them.
